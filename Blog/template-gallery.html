Style Check

Aesthetic Integrity Checklist

Ensure you cover all essential styling anchors during planning sessions to keep the theme cohesive:

  • Consistent Lighting Temperature

    Ensure all vendors match on warm/ambient bulb styling (typically 2700K temperature).

  • Harmonized Stationery Typography

    Align welcome boards, table numbers, place cards, and menus to use the same two brand fonts.

  • Floral Color Proportions

    Stick to a 60% dominant shade, 30% supporting tone, and 10% vivid metallic or accent color scheme.
